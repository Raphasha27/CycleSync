# Screenshots Guide

To capture application screenshots:

1. Open `index.html` in your browser
2. Login with credentials: `admin` / `admin`
3. Capture the following views:
   - **Login Screen** - The glassmorphism login interface
   - **Dashboard** - Stats cards and revenue chart
   - **Inventory** - Bike fleet management with search
   - **Customers** - Customer cards and database table
   - **Rental History** - Timeline view of all rentals
   - **Maintenance** - Maintenance tracking and scheduling
